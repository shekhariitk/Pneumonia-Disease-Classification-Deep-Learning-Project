About Pneumonia Detection

How It Works

Our pneumonia detection system uses a deep learning model trained on thousands of chest X-ray images to identify signs of pneumonia. The model analyzes patterns in the X-ray images that are characteristic of pneumonia infection.

What is Pneumonia?

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs. The air sacs may fill with fluid or pus, causing cough with phlegm or pus, fever, chills, and difficulty breathing.
